About Us
Our Story

Amore prides itself on being one of Toronto’s Best Italian Restaurants and remains a favourite amongst its long-standing loyal clientele.

With a long history of being in the business, Amore offers the same authentic Italian food and quality service they are known and loved for straight into your kitchen.

Amore is now able to accommodate more than ever with two private rooms, a larger dining space and separate bar area.

Amore is described in one word is what the Italians like to refer to as "love and passion", the epitome of hospitality, a warm welcome that only comes with experience, heart and lots of smiles!

Together with the whole Amore Family, We look forward to welcoming you into our kitchen and making new memories together.
